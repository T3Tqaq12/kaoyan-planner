## Anti-Patterns Verdict

**LLM Assessment:** Not a textbook AI slop dump. The emotional design is authentic — phase warning cards, breathing interlude, curated radio. The muted rosewood palette and editorial typography are coherent. Three AI-typical patterns appear at scale: side-stripe borders on 4 element types (`style.css:850,1486,1605,1644`), elastic bounce easing in 8 locations (`cubic-bezier(0.34,1.56,0.64,1)` on dock, icons, tooltips, polaroids), and tiny uppercase labels that are no-ops on Chinese text (`style.css:197,231,734,905,1256`).

**Detector Scan:** 5 categories, 14 hits. `side-tab` (3 hits) and `bounce-easing` (8 hits) confirmed. `broken-image` (1 hit, `index.html:235`) is a false positive — src filled dynamically by lightbox. `single-font` (1 hit) is a false positive — page loads 3 Google Fonts. `layout-transition` (1 hit, `style.css:360`) is acceptable for a progress bar.

**Browser Visualization:** Not available in this session. CLI scan only.

## Overall Impression

This is a genuinely personal, well-intentioned app with stronger emotional design than most production tools. The phase warning cards are the standout — practical study advice in a kind voice is exactly what "陪伴而非催促" means. The breathing interlude is original and serves real wellbeing. The core weakness is product maturity gaps: no undo, destructive import, silent failures, 100-item dropdown. These aren't taste issues; they're trust-and-reliability issues that erode daily confidence.

## What's Working

1. **Phase warning cards** — Specific, actionable study tips in a human voice. "KMP是本章唯一难点——不用死磕优化版" is authentic coaching, not generic encouragement.
2. **Breathing interlude** — A 14-second breathing exercise between checkin and celebration inserts physiological regulation into the reward cycle. In exam-prep context, this serves real wellbeing.
3. **Typography and color system** — Playfair Display + Inter + Caveat is a thoughtful 3-font hierarchy. Muted rosewood #7B5B5B on warm paper #F7F5F1 is cohesive. The 3% SVG noise texture adds tactility without noise.

## Priority Issues

### [P1] No undo after checkin
**Where:** `app.js:1110-1208` (handleCheckin)
**Why:** Primary daily action commits instantly to localStorage. A mis-tap creates permanent wrong data with no fix. Over 200+ days this WILL happen.
**Fix:** Save previous state before commit. 5-second undo toast. Add delete (× on long-press) on `.log-entry`.
→ `/impeccable harden index.html`

### [P2] Import destroys data without confirmation
**Where:** `app.js:1737-1754` (import handler)
**Why:** Clicking import → selecting a file immediately overwrites ALL data. An accidental file selection destroys months of records. No preview, no backup, no confirmation.
**Fix:** Preview modal showing detected date range. "覆盖/合并" choice. Auto-backup before overwrite.
→ `/impeccable harden index.html`

### [P3] Side-stripe border on 4 different element types
**Where:** `style.css:850, 1486, 1605, 1644`
**Why:** Same `border-left: 2px solid var(--accent)` on phase-active, history groups, section dividers, and method cards. Visual cue has collapsed — user cannot learn what it means. Detector flagged this.
**Fix:** Reserve left border for `.phase-active` only. Diversify the other three with background tints, top borders, or no-border differentiation.
→ `/impeccable quieter style.css`

### [P4] Streak drops to 1 after one missed day
**Where:** `app.js:744-747` (updateStreak)
**Why:** 47-day streak becomes 1 after a single break. Principle states "连续天数是一种记录而非评判" — current behavior IS judgement. Harsh and binary.
**Fix:** One-day grace period. Track lastMissedDate. Keep streak if only yesterday was missed, show "休息~" annotation.
→ `/impeccable harden app.js:updateStreak`

### [P5] No onboarding for first visit
**Where:** `app.js:771-783` (init), `app.js:1219-1224` (empty states)
**Why:** First load shows 4 identical empty states with no guidance. Says "you've done nothing" four times. No indication of what to do first.
**Fix:** Welcome card on first visit. Replace "🔥 0天" with "从今天开始" until first checkin.
→ `/impeccable onboard index.html`

## Persona Red Flags

**Alex (Power User):** No keyboard shortcuts. Checkin form doesn't remember last duration/mood. Desktop layout wastes 60%+ of screen.
**Casey (Distracted Mobile):** Data-action buttons ~28px (below 44px touch target). Breathing skip is low-contrast. Camera defaults to rear in crowded-transit context. No push-failure feedback.
**Riley (Stress Tester):** localStorage-full = alert() + data loss. Two tabs = no cross-tab coordination. IndexedDB unavailable = silent photo loss. Timezone change could break streaks.
**嘉敏 (Actual User):** Countdown badge identical at 180 vs 7 days. No retrospective view for 200+ days of effort. Breathing after every checkin becomes annoyance by the 50th time.

## Questions to Consider

1. What if the countdown wasn't shown? Every other UI element is about "what you did today" — does the external deadline serve or stress?
2. What if checkin didn't require a form? A single toggle with optional details — would a lower barrier increase daily consistency?
3. What if breathing happened between tomato focus and break (functionally meaningful pause) rather than between checkin and celebration (delaying validation)?
